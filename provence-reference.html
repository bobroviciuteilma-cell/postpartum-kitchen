Chicken Bone Broth

Deep iron & collagen — sip a warm cup a day.
✦ PP Gold Iron Make-ahead
Makes ~3 litres · 2½ hours
What you need
4chicken feet (more if they fit)
2chicken necks
3 litrescold water
2 tbspapple cider vinegar
¾ tspginger powder
1 tspturmeric powder
½ tspblack peppercorns
4garlic cloves, smashed
½ tspwhole ajwain seeds
2bay leaves
1 + 1sprig rosemary · sprig thyme
Saltto taste
How to make it
1
Soak. 30 min
Chicken feet, necks and water in a big pot with the cider vinegar. Sit, no heat, 30 min.
2
Boil & skim. ~15 min
Boil hard; scoop off and discard the grey foam.
3
Simmer low. 1.5–2.5 hrs
Drop to a bare simmer. Add ginger, turmeric, peppercorns, garlic, ajwain, bay, rosemary and thyme. Skim and top up with hot water now and then.
4
Season, strain & serve.
Salt to taste; strain off the bones and bits.
✦ PP Gold version
Turn it into the warming confinement broth — just add:
7 red dates, split · with the spices (step 3)
1 tbsp goji · last 15 min only
½ tsp moringa · per bowl, off the heat (never boil)
Good to know Set like jelly when chilled — that's perfect. Sip a warm cup daily, or use as a soup base. Keeps 4 days in the fridge, or freezes in portions.
